Robots in Warehousing:

Revolutionising Efficiency and Productivity

Topic Description

Implementing robots in warehousing poses various risks that need to be taken into account when implementing this technology into the Supply Chain Industry.
While employing robots can bring significant benefits to automation in terms of efficiency and productivity, it is crucial to consider the potential impacts on sustainability, including the ethical, economic and environmental aspects (Aldinhas Ferreira et al., 2017).
