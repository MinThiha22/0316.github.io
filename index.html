Automation in Warehousing

Automation has changed human society, transforming various industries and revolutionising the way we work.
From its early roots in the Ford assembly line to the modern supply chain logistics, automation has played a pivotal role in enhancing efficiency, productivity, and overall economic growth.
Nowadays, automation has become even more sophisticated. In the realm of supply chain logistics, automation has improved the way products are handled, stored, and transported.
One notable area of advancement is warehousing. Traditionally, warehousing involved manual labour for activities such as inventory management, order fulfilment, and packaging.
However, with the advent of automation technologies, such as robotics warehouses have undergone a profound transformation. (Hamberg & Verriet, 2012).
